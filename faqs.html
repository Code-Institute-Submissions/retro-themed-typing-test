FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)

This game is designed to put your typing skills to the test. The way the game works is once you press play random words are generated in random sentences in which have to be spelled out exactly correct even to the punctuation for the word to be classed as correct. Once you get to the end of each line the game will then scroll onto the next line of words to be typed. Once the game is finished and the 1 minute countdown goes to 0 the game will then calculate based on keystrokes, correct, and incorrect words what your words per minute is. You will be presented with your results which can then be shared to social media!
